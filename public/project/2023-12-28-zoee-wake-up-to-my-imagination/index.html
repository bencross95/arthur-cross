Zoee - Wake Up To My Imagination

Sep 17, 2021

Zoee’s journey as an artist has been interesting, hidden at first as the anonymous vocalist from Hey QT the iconic PC music banger. She then released a smattering of her own singles at various iconic labels including Ryan Hemsworth’s Secret Songs and Vegyn’s Plz Make It Ruins, but as an artist was not truly out of the undergrowth. In Flaw Flower her debut album however we see her given a larger space to flourish and become an artist completely in her own right. Read more here.
